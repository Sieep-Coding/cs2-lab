## **Key Storylines**

### **1. The "molodoy" Evolution**

While the world focuses on **FalleN** and **KSCERATO**, FURIA's recent climb to World #2 has been fueled by **molodoy**. His impact in the opening duels has allowed YEKINDAR and yuurih to play much more freely. If he can withstand the "XANTARES peek," FURIA should take this series.

### **2. The Turkish Aim-Star Duo**

Aurora (featuring the legendary **XANTARES** and **Wicadia**) is the tournament's dark horse. Wicadia has been posting a **1.24 multi-kill rating** over the last three months, proving he can go head-to-head with anyone in the world.

### **3. The Veteran IGL Battle**

It’s a clash of philosophies: **FalleN’s** legendary AWP-led mid-round calling versus **MAJ3R’s** structured, explosive executes. On a map like **Inferno**, this will come down to who saves their utility better for the 40-second mark.
