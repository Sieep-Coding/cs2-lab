## Overview

The Grand Final of the BLAST Bounty 2026 Season 1 Finals is here. Falcons have been the story of the tournament, reaching the final with academy stand-in **NucleonZ** while NiKo and m0NESY perform at their absolute peak.

On the other side, PARIVISION have become the "giant killers" of Malta, proving that Jame’s disciplined, slow-paced system is still a nightmare to play against on LAN.

## Map 1: Mirage

PARIVISION CT side starts strong as Jame delivers a 3 vs 1 clutch to secure  a 3-5 lead for PARIVISION, they add to it to make it 3-7.

Falcons look shaky as they finally find an answer at 5-10. nota quickly answers with an ace to make it 5-11.

PARIVISION hit 12 rounds first but Falcons stabilize through a strong A hold to make it 8-12.

PARIVISION finally crack the A site the close the map 9-13.

## Map 2: Dust2

Falcons start strong on the CT side, taking an early lead of 3-1.

PARIVISION fight back through Jame calling B site hits over and over to tie it up at 3-3.

PARIVISION continue to abuse the B site then finish with a cat hit to A, taking a 4-8 lead at half.

Falcons start their T side strong, bringing it it back to 9-9, but PARIVISION hit back to get double digits first at 9-10.

It's a back and forth affair as both teams trade rounds, Falcons continue to break the B site, leaving it at 10-11 in favor of PARIVISION.

PARIVISION close out the map with strong retake on the A site as Falcons run out of ideas.

## Map 3: Inferno

PARIVISION take the pistol round on a quick banana to B execute.

PARIVISION continue to dominate the early rounds hitting both sites successfully, taking a 0-5 lead.

Falcons finally find their footing through Niko to make it 3-6.
