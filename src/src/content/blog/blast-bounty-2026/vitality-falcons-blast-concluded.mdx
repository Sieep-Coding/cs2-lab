> Falcons win over Vitality 2-1 in a thrilling semifinal.

> Maps played: Inferno (10-13), Anubis (13-4), Mirage (6-13)

> Nick's MVP: `Niko` - Despite flameZ having a higher Rating 3.0, Niko's performance dictated the series.

## Overview
A high-stakes LAN semifinal, defined by veto leverage, star impact, and mid-round execution. 

Falcons come in with a young stand-in, looking to upset the seasoned Vitality squad.

Updates will be provided map by map as the series progresses.

## Map 1: Inferno

Vitality's T-side is off to a strong start, breaking the bomb site consistently. It is currently 3-1.

The map stays close at 10-10, with Niko delivering an ace clutch to drum up the rest of the team.

No kyosuke or zonic, no problem. Falcons hit take map 1.

## Map 2: Anubis
Vitality are off to another strong start on Anubis, starting the half 5-0.

The second half starts off with Vitality leading 9-3. flameZ is on fire at 19 frags. For Falcons, stand in Nucleonz delivered on their CT side to give them 3 rounds.

Vitality roll over Falcons to take map 2 and force a decider.

## Map 3: Mirage
Falcons are dominating Mirage early on, off to an 8-1 start.

Niko is 20-3 going into round 12 as Falcons look to close out the series quick. Zywoo is 2-11 on the other side.

Falcons finish the series strong with Niko leading the way. They take the series 2-1 over Vitality to advance to the grand finals.
